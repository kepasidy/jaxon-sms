Program description

Jaxon is a private, personal SMS reminder program operated by Kevin Jones. It sends appointment reminders and medication- and procedure-preparation reminders (for example, the timing of steps before a scheduled medical appointment) to the account owner and to individuals who have personally agreed to receive them. It is not a marketing program and sends no promotional messages.

Consent and opt-in

Recipients are added only with their direct, personal consent given to the operator. There is no public sign-up or third-party lead source; consent is not obtained from, or shared with, any third party or affiliate. By opting in, a recipient agrees to receive recurring reminder text messages from Jaxon at the mobile number they provided.

Message frequency and cost

Message frequency varies with the recipient's scheduled appointments — several messages may be sent in a day around an appointment, and none on other days. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-out and help

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message; you will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent. Reply HELP for assistance, or contact kevin@jaxonscheduler.com.

Carriers

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Message delivery is subject to your mobile carrier's transmission.

Privacy

We do not share, sell, or otherwise provide your mobile phone number or your messaging consent information to any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. For full details on how your information is handled, see our Privacy Policy.
