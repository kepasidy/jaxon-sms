Jaxon

A private, personal appointment & medication reminder service operated by Kevin Jones.

Jaxon sends SMS reminders for scheduled medical appointments and their preparation steps to the account owner and to individuals who have personally consented to receive them. It is a personal utility, not a marketing service, and sends no promotional messages.

Message program disclosures

Recurring appointment reminders. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for help.

We do not share, sell, or otherwise provide your mobile phone number or messaging consent to any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
