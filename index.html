Jaxon

Personal appointment & medication reminders — operated by Kevin Jones.

Jaxon is a small, private reminder service. It keeps track of upcoming medical appointments and the preparation steps that go with them — when to start fasting, when to take a pre-appointment medication, when to leave — and sends timely SMS reminders so nothing important is missed. It is built for one household: the account owner and a designated family member who helps keep track of care.

What Jaxon does

  • Sends reminders for scheduled medical appointments and their exact preparation timing (for example, "start drinking water 90 minutes before").
  • Tracks a course of recurring appointments (such as a radiation series) and counts progress toward completion.
  • Loops in one consented family member as a backup, so a critical reminder isn't missed if the account owner is unavailable.

Who it's for

Jaxon is a personal utility, not a marketing service and not a public product. It sends no promotional messages. Recipients are only the account owner and one family member, each of whom has personally consented to receive messages.

Message program disclosures

Jaxon sends recurring appointment & medication reminders. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for help.

We do not share, sell, or otherwise provide your mobile phone number or messaging consent to any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
